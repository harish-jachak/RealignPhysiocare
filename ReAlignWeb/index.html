About Our Clinic

The ReAlign PhysioCare clinic was established by Dr. Shubhangi Jachak & Dr. Rita Rathi with one singular objective: to provide holistic physiotherapy and rehabilitation services with deep, personalized clinical attention.

Both are highly skilled therapists with 10 years of clinical expertise spanning custom programs for Neurological, Orthopedic, and Cardiac rehabilitation.

Our Holistic Objectives:

  • Restore Physical Well-being
  • Maintain Post-Injury Quality
  • Prevent Chronic Illness
  • Major Neuro-Surgical Rehab
